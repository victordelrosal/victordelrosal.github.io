Profile

Victor del Rosal is a lecturer, professor, and innovator working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, ethics, and organizational systems. His work combines rigorous academic inquiry with practical system-building, creating frameworks that bridge theoretical research and real-world implementation.

His research focuses on the societal implications of AI systems, examining how computational technologies reshape institutions, decision-making processes, and human agency. This work manifests both as published scholarship and as deployed systems that encode ethical considerations into software architecture.
