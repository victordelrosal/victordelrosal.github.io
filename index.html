Victor del Rosal

Two decades of bridging the gap between emerging technology and practical business value.

I'm Victor del Rosal, Chief AI Officer at fiveinnolabs, where we turn generative AI from hype into practical business value. I teach AI at National College of Ireland and UCD Professional Academy.

Two books: Humanlike: the AI Transformation (2024) and Disruption: Emerging Technologies and the Future of Work (2015, used as a textbook across Ireland, Switzerland, and the US).

Background: BSc Industrial Engineering (Monterrey Tech), MSc Management (UCD Smurfit), executive programs at Oxford and Harvard. Previously: business analyst at Tata Consultancy Services, strategy lead at CloudStrong (acquired 2018).
