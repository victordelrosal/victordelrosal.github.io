I. The Premise

Thinking is no longer the moat.

If artificial intelligence out-thinks us, and it will, then sapiens ends as a self-definition. Not because the species becomes stupid. Because thinking stops being the faculty that distinguishes the species. The crown moves. The species needs a new name and nobody knows what it is yet.

Club does not claim the new name. Club is the room in which the new name gets found, together, in our bodies, in our towns, every week.

Most responses to the AGI transition are individual. Read a book. Take a course. Doomscroll a feed. Almost none are communal, embodied, repeated, ritualised. Club is the missing infrastructure. Founders' table — 3 May 2026
II. The Protocol

Six beats. Ninety minutes.

Every room, every Saturday, the same shape. Thin enough to be portable. Thick enough to be felt.

  1. I. Door

    A coin. A threshold. The crossing means something.

  2. II. Move

    A walk, a hit, a stretch. The body, before the mind.

  3. III. Play

    A cognitive game. With each other, with the machine. Scored. An artefact you walk out with.

  4. IV. Sit

    Reset. Water. Chairs in a circle. No silence ritual; just sit.

  5. V. Talk

    One question on the wall. Civic argument, not testimony. Disagreement is the format.

  6. VI. Close

    One sentence each: what you are taking out of the room. Lights off.

No silence ritual. No synchronised greeting. No sacred phrase. No founder monologue. The room takes itself seriously enough to be honest, and lightly enough to be fun.

III. The Token

Lights on.
Lights off.

At the Newport handball alley, a four-euro coin switches the lights on. The players play. The lights go off. After softball at Swinford, players Revolut two euro to cover costs.

That is the model. Honest, frictionless, scarce by design.

  • No leash. Nobody owns a seat. Nobody renews. Nobody churns.
  • Honest economics. A few euro is a price a human pays without thinking.
  • Built-in scarcity. The session ends. The next one earns its own coin.
  • No retention engineering. The session itself is the retention strategy.
  • Threshold, not tax. The coin in the hand is the ritual. Keep it physical.
