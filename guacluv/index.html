Good to know

Questions, answered.

How does the ripe-day guarantee work?

Tell us the day you plan to eat, and we ripen each avocado to hit peak on that date. If it arrives under- or over-ripe, snap a photo and we replace it free — no questions.

Where do you deliver?

We're launching in Ireland first (Dublin, Cork, Galway) and rolling across the UK and EU through the founding year. Join the waitlist and we'll prioritise your city.

Can I pause or skip?

Anytime, from your account. Going on holiday? Pause. Big taco week? Add a kit. Your subscription bends to your life.

Where do the avocados come from?

From a small network of regenerative growers we partner with directly — fair prices to farmers, tasting notes to you. Origins rotate with the harvest so you always get peak-season fruit.

Is the packaging really compostable?

Yes — moulded-pulp trays and home-compostable wrap, zero single-use plastic. Delivery emissions are measured and offset on every order.
