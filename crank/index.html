THE MECHANISM

Strip the mystique and every agent loop is four parts.

The prompt is the cheap part now. The art is almost entirely in parts three and four.

01 · GENERATOR

The next prompt

A template, a task list, or a mutation of the last attempt. In Crank, the director writes its own brief and binary criteria before spending a token.

02 · AGENT RUN

The work

A fleet of specialists, one per independent workstream, each opening with a three-line readback so misalignment dies at line three, not in the artifact.

03 · EVALUATOR

The cold verifier

A separate agent, fresh context every round, sees only the criteria and the artifacts, and is instructed to fail the work. For anything runnable, it drives the artifact like a user.

04 · CONTROLLER

The decision

Investigate why a criterion failed, then iterate structurally, fork rival candidates when stuck, or stop honestly. The verifier's verdict gates the stop, not the builder's feelings.

“A loop with a weak evaluator fails confidently a thousand times and bills you for it.”

THE DISCIPLINE

Every pin on the cylinder is a rule that earned its place.

Crank is benchmarked against the field and upgraded under hostile verification. Each rule traces to a named, fetch-verified source.

The verifier gates the stop

The loop cannot declare done while any binary criterion fails. Self-grading is systematically lenient; the grader lives in a separate context window.

Anthropic loop experiments · CMA Outcomes

The evaluation surface is frozen

Criteria, check scripts and verifier instructions are part of the contract. A fix that edits the test instead of the work is a downgrade in disguise.

Karpathy autoresearch · Anthropic harness law

Ground before you frame

Locate the real artifacts and write an explain-back, every claim marked VERIFIED or ASSUMED, before the contract freezes. The expensive bugs are shared misunderstanding.

Uriostegui · ClineFlow

Baseline before you improve

Measure the unmodified starting state first. Every claim of improvement is proved against the baseline, not against memory of it.

Karpathy autoresearch

Memory that compounds

Verified rules distill to LEARNINGS; reusable scripts promote to a tools library. Run N+1 starts from competence, not from scratch.

Lance Martin · Voyager · AWM

Freedom stops at the gate

Money, reach, publishing, deletion: anything irreversible pauses for a human, or parks in an approval queue when running unattended. The gate never lowers because no one is watching.

Crank doctrine · scheduled mode

TWO WAYS TO TURN IT

Present between rounds, or asleep while it runs.

INTERACTIVE

/crank

You hand over the wheel: it grasps intent, writes the contract, dispatches the fleet, red-teams, iterates. Gates pause in-session and wait for you.

  • ORIENT → FRAME → DECOMPOSE → EXECUTE → RED-TEAM → DECIDE
  • Binary criteria frozen at FRAME, downgrades always surfaced
  • Fresh cold verifier every round
SCHEDULED

/crank scheduled

The same loop with the human out of the room. It has to survive a closed laptop and a cold context, so three things become mandatory.

  • Gates become an async approval queue, never auto-approval
  • A hard budget ceiling that aborts, not asks nicely
  • Crash-safe STATE.json: a cron cold-start resumes mid-run

PROOF OF DISCIPLINE

On 4 July 2026, the loop upgraded itself.

Crank was pointed at Crank: benchmark your own operating manual against 23 approaches, upgrade, and ship only what a hostile reviewer passes. The full run record is public in the repo.

6f0ee70 · ROUND 1

Absorbed Uriostegui and ClineFlow: the grounding discipline, the run journal, the builder readback. Also caught its own manual citing a log no section defined, and its public repo three weeks behind the installed skill.

d9f0e9a · ROUND 2

The field benchmark: Karpathy's autoresearch, the Ralph loop, Anthropic's harnesses, Reflexion, LATS, Voyager, GEPA, AlphaEvolve and more. Six new mechanisms adopted; six fashionable ideas rejected with written rationale, including the never-stop directive.

5a0c3b3 · THE RECORD

README, benchmark, bet-weights and both verifier verdicts, published. Nothing quantified from unverified coverage.

The second verifier caught the loop recording a fix it had not yet written. The catch, and the correction, are preserved in the run log verbatim. That is the design working: a check the loop cannot talk its way past, even when the work being checked is its own upgrade.
