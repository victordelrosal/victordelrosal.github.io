1. Apple Will Unveil Gemini-Powered Siri Assistant in February

The integration aims to make Siri more capable at complex tasks and follows Google's broader AI partnerships, including a strategic investment in Sakana AI, a Japanese startup exploring alternative AI architectures beyond transformers.
