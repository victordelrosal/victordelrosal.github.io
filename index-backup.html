Profile

Victor del Rosal is Chief AI Officer and Founder at fiveinnolabs, an AI innovation consultancy focused on turning emerging generative AI capabilities into practical, high-impact solutions for businesses. He lectures in the MSc in AI for Business at National College of Ireland and teaches professional AI programs at UCD, where he brings together academic rigor and real-world implementation experience.

Author of two books on technology transformation—Humanlike: the AI Transformation (exploring the intersection of generative AI and business) and Disruption: Emerging Technologies and the Future of Work (used as a textbook in universities across Ireland, Switzerland, and the United States). His work focuses on AI education, strategy, and the practical challenges of organizational AI adoption.

With a background spanning business analysis at Tata Consultancy Services, strategy leadership at CloudStrong (acquired 2018), and over a decade of teaching innovation and emerging technologies, he combines engineering training (BSc Industrial Engineering, Monterrey Tech), business education (MSc Management, UCD Smurfit), and executive credentials (Oxford, Harvard) with hands-on AI implementation experience. Recipient of multiple teaching awards including the 2021 Teaching Hero Award and 2019 NCI President's Award for Distinguished Teaching.
